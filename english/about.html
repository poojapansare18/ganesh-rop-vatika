About Ganesh Rop Vatika

Where Passion for Plants Meets Quality & Trust

Our Story

Ganesh Rop Vatika, located in Yawat, Daund, has been a trusted name for high-quality plants and saplings for many years. What started as a small family nursery has now grown into a large, well-maintained plant production farm with 500+ varieties.

Our mission is to spread greenery, help farmers, garden lovers, and communities grow healthy plants, and promote a clean environment.
